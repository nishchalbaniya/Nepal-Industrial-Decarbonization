Day 6 v0.6.0 — Hetauda cooler 3D STEP file

Generated · v0.6.0 = v0.5.0 calibrated model + FreeCAD 1.1.1 STEP export. The .step file is a real ISO 10303-21 AP214 file (62 KB, 53 faces, 11 parts). Open it in FreeCAD, Onshape, Fusion 360, or any STEP-compliant CAD viewer.

What's in the .step file:
  • Main cooler housing (33.2 m long x 3.9 m wide x 2.2 m tall outer; hollowed inner for the grate volume)
  • 6 compartment partitions (vertical plates at 5.46 m intervals along the X axis)
  • 1 sec-air duct stub (1.0 m diameter, at the kiln end)
  • 1 exhaust duct stub (0.8 m diameter, at the cold end)
  • 1 kiln-discharge coupling (1.5 m diameter, vertical, at the hot end)
  • 1 clinker-discharge chute (1.2 m wide, at the cold end)
  • 1 grate plate (full length, 0.78 m bed depth from the top of the housing)

The geometry is the v0.5.0 calibrated posterior: 32.8 m grate length, 0.78 m bed depth, 6 compartments. This is close to the real Hetauda plant geometry (~32 m grate, ~0.7 m bed, 5 compartments) and is the result of the Day 5 v0.5.0 calibration on the consistent synthetic Hetauda target (loss 234 -> 5.0, 98% reduction).
