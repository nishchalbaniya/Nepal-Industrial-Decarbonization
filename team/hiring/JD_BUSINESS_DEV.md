# Job Description: Business Developer — Part-time → Full-time

## Company
**Himalayan Carbon Nepal** — Open-source industrial decarbonization platform. Hetauda Cement pilot verified ($22.5M NPV at EU ETS). First 5 customers in 90 days.

## Role
**Part-time Business Developer (B2B sales + partnerships)** — 3 days/week, 6-month initial contract → full-time after first paying customer.

## What you'll do
- Build lead list of 100+ Nepali cement plants and 200+ brick kilns
- Send 30+ personalized outreach emails/week
- Book 5+ discovery calls/week
- Run free PoC engagements (2-week, 1 success metric)
- Close deals: $5-20k/yr per plant, 3-12 month contracts
- Build partnerships: NCMA, Department of Environment, NEA, World Bank Nepal, GIZ, GCF
- Apply for grants: GIZ, GCF, ADB, World Bank ($200k-500k targets)
- Manage CRM (HubSpot free or Notion)
- Co-author pitch deck, case studies, blog posts

## What you bring
- 5+ years B2B sales in Nepal, India, or South Asia
- Track record of: closing industrial SaaS deals $10k+/yr
- Network: at least 10 contacts in Nepali cement, brick, or industrial sector
- Skills: cold email, discovery calls, proposal writing, CRM, grant writing
- Bonus: technical background, can read Python or financial models
- Languages: English + Nepali (Hindi a plus)

## Compensation
- **Base**: NPR 150,000 / month (~$1,150 USD) for 3 days/week
- **Commission**: 10% of Year 1 contract value (e.g., $5k deal = $500 to you; $50k deal = $5k)
- **Performance bonus**: NPR 100k per closed grant (e.g., $200k GCF grant = NPR 100k)
- **Equity**: 1-2% of the company (4-year vest, 1-year cliff)
- Travel reimbursed
- Co-authorship on case studies

## First 90 days
- Month 1: 50 outreach emails, 10 calls, 3 demos, 1 free PoC signed
- Month 2: 1 closed deal ($5-20k), 1 grant application submitted
- Month 3: 2 more closed deals, 1 partnership signed (NCMA or DoE)
