# Job Description: Graphics Designer (Brand + Pitch) — Project

## Company
**Himalayan Space Solutions** — Open-source industrial decarbonization platform. Need a brand that looks as world-class as the technology.

## Role
**Project-based Graphics Designer** — 2-week initial project (brand identity + pitch deck + marketing site visuals). After: ongoing per-asset basis.

## What you'll produce (week 1)
- Logo (3 concepts, then refine to 1)
- Brand identity guide (color palette, typography, voice, don'ts)
- SVG favicon + GitHub badge

## Week 2
- Pitch deck (12 slides, designed)
- Marketing site hero section (5 SVG illustrations)
- 3 social media templates (LinkedIn, Twitter, square)
- Case study 1-pager (Hetauda)

## What you bring
- 5+ years brand / graphic design
- Portfolio with at least 3 B2B tech or climate brand projects
- Comfortable with: Figma, SVG, web fonts
- Style: minimal, engineered, modern (think Stripe, Linear, Watershed)
- Bonus: motion / video for the 2-min demo reel
- Fluent in English

## Compensation
- **Project fee**: NPR 200,000-400,000 (~$1,500-3,000 USD) for 2 weeks
- **Ongoing**: NPR 10,000-30,000 per asset (slide, banner, illustration, video)
- **Bonus**: NPR 50,000 if pitch deck helps close a $50k+ deal
- Co-credit on the brand

## How to apply
Email nishchal.baniya@himalayancarbonnepal.com with:
1. Portfolio (3-5 brand projects)
2. 1-paragraph: "How would you visually represent 'Nepal + climate + industrial' in 1 image?"
3. LinkedIn

Interview: 30-min call + small paid test (1 logo concept, NPR 5,000).
Decision: 5 days.

## Why this role
- **Visible impact**: every plant manager and investor sees your work
- **High pay for short project**: 2 weeks, paid well
- **Brand-led climate work**: rare in Nepal
- **Ongoing**: per-asset basis if we like the work
