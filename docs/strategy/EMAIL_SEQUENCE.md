# Email Nurture Sequence — Free → Paid in 60 Days

> 5 emails, sent over 60 days, to every new signup on
> `nepalcarbon.org.np`. The goal is to convert a Free (Express)
> signup into a Pro (Full) customer by **Day 60**, or at minimum
> to have them ready to convert in the next quarter.

> **Audience:** plant managers, plant owners, kiln operators, NCMA
> staff, and DoE officials who have created a tenant on the platform
> but have not yet paid.

> **Voice:** "trusted Nepali engineer friend." No "synergy", no
> "unlock", no "delight", no exclamation marks in the subject line.
> Short sentences. One CTA per email. Bilingual sign-off in the
> first and last email.

> **From name:** "Nishchal at Himalayan Carbon". Not "the team",
> not "no-reply". A real name on a real inbox.

> **Cadence:**
> - Email 1 — Day 0 (immediately after signup, transactional welcome)
> - Email 2 — Day 3 (after first dashboard visit, behavioural)
> - Email 3 — Day 10 (after first data entry, value-reinforcement)
> - Email 4 — Day 25 (pricing + Pro offer, conversion)
> - Email 5 — Day 50 (last-chance + concrete next step, urgency)

> **Each email is 100 words max in the body** (excluding subject,
> preheader, CTA, and sign-off). Plain-text style. Link, not button.

## Operational notes (for the founder and Marketing Lead)

- **The 100-word body is a hard cap.** If you cannot say it in
  100 words, the email is not ready. Plant managers delete long
  emails.
- **The CTA is one link, not five.** One job per email.
- **Plain text > HTML.** These emails should look like a real
  person wrote them at a real desk. A small logo at the bottom
  is fine. No banner images, no marketing headers.
- **Bilingual sign-off in Email 1 and Email 5 only.** It marks
  the start and end of the relationship, doesn't repeat in the
  middle.
- **Reply handling is the moat.** Every reply to this sequence
  is read by Nishchal personally for the first 6 months. Speed
  matters more than script.
- **Suppression:** anyone who has already replied to any email
  in the sequence is removed from the next send. We don't
  email people who are already talking to us.
- **Metrics that matter:** Day-3 activation rate (target 60%),
  Day-10 reply rate (target 8%), Day-25 → Pro conversion (target
  5%), Day-60 cohort conversion (target 3%). If any of these
  miss, the next iteration tightens the email above it.
