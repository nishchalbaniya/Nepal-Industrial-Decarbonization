Nepal's industrial transition is happening without measurement.

Six million tonnes of CO₂ a year from cement. Two million from brick. Zero ISO 14064 compliance. Zero Verra registrations. Zero SBTi targets. The world is moving to a carbon-priced economy — and Nepali plants have no baseline to even begin.

EU CBAM arrives 2026

Cement exported to the EU will be tariffed based on its carbon intensity. Plants without verified baselines lose market access.

2

Banks require PCAF

By 2027, international banks will require financed emissions disclosure (PCAF) before lending. Most Nepali plants have nothing.

Carbon credits = revenue
